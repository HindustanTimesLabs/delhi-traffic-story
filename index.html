Delhi crawls as vehicles rule the roads

By Gurman Bhatia and Soumya Pillai

15th December, 2016

The time spent by Delhi residents on the roads has doubled in the last six years and traffic speeds have reduced by half during peak hours, a study carried out by six road design experts and engineers has found.
